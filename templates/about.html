How ResumeAI Works

A college project built to help job seekers optimize their resumes for ATS systems

The ATS Problem

Over 75% of resumes never reach a human recruiter because they fail Applicant Tracking System (ATS) filters. These automated systems scan resumes for keywords, formatting, and structure before any human sees them.

ResumeAI simulates how an ATS evaluates your resume, giving you a detailed score and specific suggestions to improve your chances. You can even paste a job description and we’ll tell you how closely your resume matches it, along with keywords you may be missing.
